Your weekly intake of the Denver Music Scene is here!

Another week, and another amazing line-up of shows in Denver. Things kick off tonight with local legends The Fray at Red Rocks. Free tickets were going around. Did you get yours?

Red Bull (yes the energy drink) is hosting two events that you should probably check out - Tuesday is Blood Road a Mountain Biking film and Thursday is Pomo, a live electronic artist. Both are at the Bluebird and tickets are CHEAP. Umphrey’s McGee, Conor Oberst (previously your favorite emo singer/writer Bright Eyes), MisterWives, and Zeds Ded are all this week as well. How spoiled are we!?

Get after it Denver!
